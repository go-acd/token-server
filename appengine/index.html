Amazon Cloud Drive login for go-acd

This website allows you to download Amazon OAuth credentials (access_token, refresh-token and token_type).

When you're ready to proceed, please click on "Download my credentials" below. It will take you to Amazon website and ask you to login with go-acd.

After you login with Amazon, you'll immediately receive acd-token.json which can be used by go-acd implementers to access your Amazon Cloud Drive account. This website never log, save or in anyway interact with Amazon on your behalf beyond providing you with a token and refreshing that token for you. Please keep the acd-token.json secure.

The default location for acd-token.json

  • Linux: ~/.config/acd-token.json
  • Mac: ~/.config/acd-token.json

If you believe your credentials were compromised or you simply no longer wish to be logged in with this service, you may remove the login permission through your Amazon account Amazon.com > Your Account > Manage Login with Amazon.

If you are experiencing any issues with this service, please contact me.
